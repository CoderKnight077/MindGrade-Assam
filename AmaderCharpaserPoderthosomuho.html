Write a letter to the editor of a local English daily news paper for bad condition of road in your locality

To
The Editor,
Assam Tribune,
Guwahati- 3,


Subject: Urgent Need for Road Repairs in New colony , Lumding

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am writing to highlight the deplorable condition of the roads in our locality, New colony , Lumding. The main road
is filled with potholes and uneven surfaces, causing damage to vehicles and posing serious safety risks. During the rainy season,
these potholes become invisible hazards, leading to frequent accidents.
The poor road condition affects daily commuting, especially for those relying on public transportation,
which often avoids this route due to its state. Despite several complaints to the municipal authorities, no substantial action has been taken. The temporary patchwork repairs are inadequate.
We urge the authorities to prioritize the repair and proper maintenance of the roads. Highlighting this issue in your esteemed newspaper could expedite
the necessary improvements. The residents of New Colony deserve safe and well-maintained roads.
Thank you for your attention to this matter.
Yours sincerely,
‘xyz’
