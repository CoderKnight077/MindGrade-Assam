NOTES

1. Let’s see how much we have understood :


(a) Where was Anne Frank from?

Ans : Anne Frank was from the Netherlands.

(b) How has the voice of Anne Frank reached out across continents and generations?

Ans :- The voice of Anne Frank reached out across continents and generations through her diary.

(c) What did Anne Frank see lying on the table among her birthday presents ?


Ans: Anne Frank saw a bunch of roses, a plant and some peonies lying on the table among her birthday presents.

(d) Why did Anne Frank remain in her bed lying till a quarter to seven on her birthday?


Ans: Because it was 6 O’ clock she was not allowed to get up that hour. That’s why she remains in her bed lying till a quarter to seven on her birthday.



(c) Who does ‘you’ in the lesson refer to?


Ans :- ‘You’ in the lesson refers to Annie’s dairy.

(f) What is ‘Camera obscura’?


Ans:- Camera obscura is a darkened room with a small hole or lens at one side through which an image is projected into a wall or table opposite the hole.

(g) Who welcomed Anne in the dining room.


Ans: Anne received a warm welcome from her cat name called Moor tie.



(h) Why does Anne Frank think that it is an odd idea for someone like her to keep a diary?

Ans:- Anne Frank thinks that it is an odd idea for someone like her to keep a diary because she has never written a diary before and she does not think that other people will be interested in what a thirteen year girl has to say.

(i) How does Anne Frank describe her feelings in one of her slightly melancholy days?

Ans :- In her melancholy days Anne Frank while sitting chin in hand feels too bored and thinks about whether she should go out or stay at home.



(j) Who is kitty?


Ans:- Anne called her diary as her best friend whom she can tell everything about and gave a name to the diary called kitty. -->
